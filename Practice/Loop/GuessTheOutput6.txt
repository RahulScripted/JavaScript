What does the following code snippet do?

for (let x = 1; x <= 10; x++) {
  console.log(2 * x);
}


Select one of the following options:

1.  Prints even numbers from 1 to 10

2.  Prints numbers from 1 to 10

3.  Prints multiples of 2 from 2 to 20

4.  Prints numbers from 2 to 20





Answer: 3.  Prints multiples of 2 from 2 to 20
