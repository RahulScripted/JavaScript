What does the break statement do in the below provided loop?

for (let i = 1; i <= 10; i++) {
    console.log(i);
    if (i === 5) break;
}


Select one of the following options:

1.  Exits the loop when i is equal to 5

2.  Continues to the next iteration when i is equal to 5

3.  Skips the loop entirely

4.  Prints numbers from 0 to 10 and skip 5





Answer: 1.  Exits the loop when i is equal to 5
