How do you print the numbers 1 to 4 on the same line with spaces, and 5 on newline ?Select correct options from the following :

1.  console.log("1 2 3 4\n5");

2.  console.log("1", "2", "3", "4\n5");

3.  console.log("1 2 3 4", "\n5");

4.  console.log("1 2 3 4"); 
    console.log("5");




Answers: 

1.  console.log("1 2 3 4\n5");

2.  console.log("1", "2", "3", "4\n5");

3.  console.log("1 2 3 4", "\n5");

4.  console.log("1 2 3 4"); 
    console.log("5");
