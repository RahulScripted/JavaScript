What does the following code achieve?

console.log(5);
console.log(6);
console.log(7);


Select one of the following options:

1.  Prints three numbers on separate lines

2.  Prints three numbers on the same line

3.  Prints one number on the first line, two numbers on the next line

4.  Throws a compilation error




Answer: 1.  Prints three numbers on separate lines
